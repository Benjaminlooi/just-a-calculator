# Privacy Policy for Just a Calculator

This privacy policy describes how the "Just a Calculator" browser extension handles user data.

## 1. No Data Collection or Transmission
"Just a Calculator" is designed with privacy as a priority. 
- We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, browsing history, website content, or analytical data to any remote servers or third parties.
- All processes occur strictly on your local device.

## 2. Local Storage Usage
The extension uses the browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) for a single, narrow purpose:
- To maintain a running tally of modified AI buzzwords (e.g., the number of times "AI" was changed to "Calculator").
- This scoreboard count is stored entirely locally within your browser profile and is never sent off your device.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the policy file in this repository.

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the repository owner or open an issue on the project repository.
